Problems in Current Payment?

Lack of Privacy

Full transparency exposes customer purchases and merchant revenues.

Auditability Gap

Existing zk-payment privacy solutions hide sender, receiver, and amount, preventing transaction verification by auditors.

High Gas Fees

Excessive fees make micro-payments impractical

Limited Interoperability

Payments are confined within isolated blockchains, restricting seamless cross-chain commerce.

Our Solution: Privacy and Auditability Combined

Auditable ZK

zk-proofs conceal sender, receiver, and amount from the public blockchain.

Built-in auditable keys enable regulators and authorized auditors to verify transactions without compromising privacy.

Aggregated Proof

Aggregates multiple zk-transactions into a single proof for gas efficiency.

Users pay zero gas fees as decentralized aggregators batch transactions, drastically reducing costs.

Cross chain

Smart contracts deployed on major blockchains enable cross-chain payments.

A unified ledger supports seamless, low-cost payments across multiple chains, overcoming interoperability barriers.
